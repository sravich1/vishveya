Vishveya's first generation controller "Vish" gives you the option to manually control or automate all areas of your home. The "Vish" controller can be easily integrated to your existing wiring. Therefore, you can still manually push a button to switch devices on or off or you can use the Vishveya smart home App on your smartphone or tablet to control the same connected devices. Whether you are at home or away, no matter where you are, you can always monitor and control all your connected devices via the internet cloud.

The Vishveya smart home system is made to simplify your life and add comfort and peace of mind.With the addition of smart sensors, you can monitor light levels, control temperature or receive alerts from smart devices, detect motion, monitor energy consumption, etc…

Our system is flexible and scalable to meet your needs today and in the future. You can start with a base system and over time you can add our smart sensors to provide additional monitoring and control to make your daily life a little easier.

Seniors living independently can live more confidently knowing they can get help whenever they need with just a press of a button. The "Vish" controller can provide health monitoring and medical alert features that can prove invaluable for seniors living alone and provide peace of mind for their loved ones. Imagine a scenario in the home where you become immobilized.Slip and fall accidents can happen anytime and anywhere in the home. Now imagine being able to get help with just the press of a button. A wearable waterproof pendant or wristband can provide 24/7 access to help and a little peace of mind. With just the press of a button, an alert will be broadcasted to all of your family members and loved ones. As soon as a family member or loved one acknowledges the alert, a green LED indicator will let the injured person know that help is on the way. In addition to medical alert, other health monitoring sensors can monitor things like heart rate, pulse, body temperature, oxygen levels on a continual basis throughout the day. This data can be uploaded to the cloud and analyzed by software. Alerts can be sent out for any abnormal patterns that are detected.
